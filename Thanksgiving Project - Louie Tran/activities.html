Arts & Crafts

The whole family is able to participate and bond together with this activity as you will be given materials to create a thanksgiving themed object. Our judges will score among the ones turned in and the winning family or individual will be given a $200 prize. The criteria is based on looks, cleanliness, and structure.

Photo Booth

You will be able to take wacky pictures with your family and will be given the pictures right afterwards. There are items availiable to put on during the pictures such as, glasses, wigs, and holdable items. Our photographer will be happy to take multiple photos for you or on your phone incase you want them personally.

Board Games

There are a group of tables set up that house the board games and give children the time to calm down and play with their friends on a multitude of games. Additionally, there are a couple of family games to help them bond and enjoy spending time with each other at the event.

Karaoke

We have a stage in the back of the field that people can come to and show their talents at singing. Our requirements for song requests is that they're not explicit and appropiate for all ages. The field will be only open until 6pm afterwards we will use the stage to present any prizes and announce our current sponsors

Scavenger Hunt

Kids will be able to go around the campus to try to find certain cards that they use to fill in the list. Our staff members are moderating around the campus to ensure that the safety of children is guarenteed. Every 2 hours there will be a drawing amoung the kids that have completed the list and will be given some prizes to take home!
