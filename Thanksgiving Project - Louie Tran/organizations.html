Fellowship Housing

The Fellowship Housing gives support to the families in need and homelessness. They have a two-year program that temporarially gives a safe place to live in with one-on-one mentoring and financial training (Designed to increase income and savings). After ther two year program has passed they assists in securing permanent housing.

Bridge of Hope

The Bridge of Hope is a nonprofit organization that helps families find housing and gives advice on financial and emotional topics. They specifically support families that are facing homelessness. Programs last to around 18 to 24 months; this allows for ample time for them to make long-term changes within their household.

Feeding America

They are a nationwide network of food banks that engage in the country to end hunger. They help feed more than 46 million people through food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other means. They rely on volunteers and donations to keep running meal programs for their locations and teach the public about the problems arising within the country.

Family Promise

Family Promise helps low-income familes achieve finanical independence. They have an, on average, 74% of families finding housing within nine weeks of entering the program and annually rotate through 200 or so individuals. Most families that have difficulty balancing their finances would benefit the most out of this program.
